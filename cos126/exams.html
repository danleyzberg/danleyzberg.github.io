EXAMS

Grading.

Your exams score will make up 50% of your grade. There are four exams in this course: (1) One written exam and (2) one programming exam midway through the semester, and (3) a written exam and (4) programming exam towards the end of the semester. There is no final.

Written Exam 2

The second written exam is tentatively scheduled for December 8th, and will cover all of the material in lecture as well as the first eight programming assignments.
