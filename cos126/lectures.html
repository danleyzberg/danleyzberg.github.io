Lectures

On this page you can find the lecture slides, required reading, and demo programs for you to play with in order to better understand the content of the lectures.

Advice. We strongly suggest that you skim the required reading before coming to lecture and that you read it thoroughly soon afterwards. We also recommend printing the slides below before lecture so you can annotate them during.

Errata. Alas, we are not perfect. In that spirit, we maintain a list of known errors in the slides, textbook, and online chapters.
